What LeaderStack Is

A web-based academic ERP focused strictly on operational integrity.

Auth Without Compromise

Email and password only. No Google login. No social providers. Identity proves who you are. Role mapping defines what you can do.

Academic Year Isolation

When a class moves to 2026, history remains untouched. No overwriting. No corruption. Academic history is sacred.

Manual Attendance

LeaderStack does not auto-mark. Teachers check present or absent. Attendance reflects real human control, not automated assumptions.

Pure Rankings

Ranking is based only on percentage. No behavioral metrics. No subjective fields. Simple. Transparent. 1st, 2nd, 3rd.

Fee & Weakness Alerts

Integrated alerts for due fees and performance dips. They simply inform the teacher. LeaderStack respects human judgment.

Data Integrity

Deletion is permanent. No restore. No rollback. This enforces responsibility and careful data management.
